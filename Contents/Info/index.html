Cell Phone Lot

The Best Way to Wait for Arriving Travelers

BWI Marshall Airport has a free cell phone lot for patrons waiting for a phone call from an arriving passenger. By using the lot, motorists can avoid circling BWI Marshall Airport roadways while waiting for incoming passengers.

Cell Phone Lot Regulations
  • No commercial or courtesy vehicles
  • No unattended vehicles
  • 1 hour maximum wait time
  • Where is the Cell Phone Lot?
Where is the Cell Phone Lot?

The BWI Marshall Airport Cell Phone Lot is located on Scott Road just to the right of the Daily Parking Garage entrance.
